About

Shenzhen International Workshop on Several Complex Variables and CR geometry 2024 is a five day event which will take place at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China from Sep 2 to Sep 6, 2024.

The organizers gratefully acknowledge the support by Shenzhen International Center for Mathematics and Department of Mathematics of Shenzhen International Center for Mathematics.

    Registration

    All are welcome. However, due to space restrictions, we kindly need you to register. Some financial support is available. To register and to apply for funding, please write to (Ilja Kossovskij) before May 31, 2024.

    Information for Visa, Travel, Lodging, and Special Needs

    We recommend applying for an F visa as it is more relevant and requires fewer documents. For an invitation letter and contact information, please contact Ms Wu.

    More information about Chinese visa.

    The SUSTech campus can be reached from the Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport (40m taxi) or Shenzhen North Railway Station (10m taxi). Hong Kong is close to Shenzhen (1h shuttle) and has more international flight options. We will arrange pickups at the Hong Kong International Airport to the SUSTech campus on Sep 1. We will also make arrangements for you to return to that airport after the workshop. There is no need for a separate visa for Hong Kong.

    For registered participants, we have reserved a block of complimentary rooms at the Best Sleep International Hotel near SUSTech campus (check in Sep 1, check out Sep 7).

    If you have any special needs (child care, dietary restrictions, etc.), please contact Ms. Wu.
